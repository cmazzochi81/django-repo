WordPress eCommerce

PHP Web App

This was my first web app, developed in 2014. The central theme of all my apps is my art work. My paintings specifically. I view the web space as a platform to display or exhibit my art, as well as demonstrate my understanding and use of whatever technology or framework I decided to use for the project. This app is similar to Flickr (it was 2014...), and it allows a user to create an account and upload photos to a photo gallery.
